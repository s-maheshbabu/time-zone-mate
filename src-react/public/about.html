TIMEZONEMATE

About

TimeZoneMate is designed to address two use cases:

World Clock

Lets you keep track of the current time in multiple locations/timezones at a glance.

As a software developer that worked with clients across several locations I always needed to know the current time in different client locations — to call people at the right time or make software deployments when everyone is asleep. Instead of googling "time in cityX" every time, I built an app that lets you add as many locations and timezones as you want.

Time Converter

Aids in converting time across multiple timezones.

When interrelated systems across different timezones start acting up, I needed a way to know what the local time was in each timezone at a given moment. TimeZoneMate has two distinct advantages:

  • Converts time across multiple timezones simultaneously, not just two.
  • Makes entry easy — type a time in any clock and all others update instantly.
